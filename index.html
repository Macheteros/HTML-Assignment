Drone Soccer

Drone soccer is a fast growing sub sector of the drone industry. Like
FPV (First Person Video) drones that are exciting and fun to fly, drone soccer
is also quite fun to engage in albeit they are not as fast as fpv drones. My
particular excitement about drone soccer is that the drones are custom builds.
Meaning that, one can build and program them from scratch, giving one the
opportunity to learn the inner workings of a drone. This to me is beyound
amazing. This technology is quickly gaining ground all over the world with
Nigeria having her first ever drone soccer competition courtesy of Global
Air Academy which is a drone company based in Lagos with international reach.
